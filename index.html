About TimerCoin


TimerCoin is a crypto currency that is based on your time. TimerCoin can only be mined using a CPU and one of the CPU's cores. This way mining the coin is a slower, less power and compute intensive process.

TimerCoin was originally setup this way so supply wasn't too large in comparison to the demand, as TimerCoin is a fairly easy coin to mine relative to other crypto's.

Get Started with TimerCoin


To get started with TimerCoin, all you have to do is download the TimerCoin client. Within the TimerCoin client you can mine TimerCoin, and send TimerCoin (As well as some other features)
